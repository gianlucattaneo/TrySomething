Buckman's Ski and Snowboard Shops — Your Mom & Pop Ski and Snowboard Shop

Whether you're a beginner, intermediate or expert skier or snowboarder, Buckman's Ski and Snowboard Shops is the best place to get the outdoor gear, apparel and accessories you need. We carry only the best products for men, women and kids.

Buckman's Ski and Snowboard Shops was founded in 1971 by Leon Buckman, who opened up our first shop in Perkiomenville, PA. This local store soon earned its name as one of the area's best ski shops.

Buckman's continues to earn that reputation today. We've grown to seven retail stores and an easy-to-shop online store too, with more than 425 employees. We're also still a family-owned and operated business — although Nancy and Jeff semi-retired in 2017, they passed the baton to their son Brad Buckman. Brad has worked in the shop since he was a teen and is just as passionate as his parents.

Our family loves skiing and hopes to provide the area's skiing, snowboarding and winter enthusiasts with the best products and gear available today. Learn More>>

Items We Carry at Buckman's Ski and Snowboard Shop

We carry a wide selection of products, from equipment to apparel to accessories. If you're a beginner, we can help you find all the essential gear you'll need to get started, including:

Ski Gear and Equipment

Buckman's is your one-stop-shop for all your skiing needs. We offer durable, expertly-designed skis built for men, women and children. We also offer skis designed for the park as well as freeride skiing. 

Snowboard Gear and Equipment

When it's time to hit the slopes, Buckman's is there. We carry a variety of snowboards, including all-mountain, backcountry, freestyle and youth.

Ski and Snowboard Boots

The right pair of snowboard or ski boots is just as important as the right board and skis when it comes time to play. Browse our selection of winter boots for premium options like the Burton Driver X Snowboard Boots or Nordica Stryder 120 DYN Boots for skiers.

Ski Bindings

Look through our range of adult and child ski bindings to find the right design for you. With the highest-quality brands in the business — from Atomic to Marker — our ski equipment is long-lasting and reliable. 

Ski Poles

Our selection of ski poles features selections that are as sturdy and reliable as they are easy to grip. Browse our options to find the right pole for your environment and skiing style.

Apparel for the Whole Family

We offer winter weather apparel for men, women and children designed to keep you warm and comfortable no matter the weather. Our impressively branded apparel selection includes jackets, mid and base layers, pants, hoodies, jeans, rain gear, shorts and much more.

Accessories 

Head to toe — from ski locks to warm ski socks — we've got you covered. Browse our selection for must-have accessories, like skincare products, to protect your skin from the elements, or Burton Total Impact shorts to protect your body in the case of a fall.

Buckman's Ski and Snowboard Shops is a real one-stop shop. You don't need to go anywhere else to find everything you need for your next ski or snowboarding trip.

Why Choose Buckman's Ski and Snowboard Shops?

In addition to our huge selection, expert staff and convenient shopping options, a few other benefits to shopping with us include:

  • A guaranteed price match if you find a lower price anywhere else
  • Free shipping on most orders of $50 or more
  • Support from a green company aiming to reduce its effect on the environment 
  • Rental packages and rates for ski and snowboard equipment

Buckman's Ski and Snowboard Shops strives to help you find the best products for your lifestyle. We love sharing our love of skiing, snowboarding and being outdoors — we hope to see you soon! Feel free to contact us with any questions.
