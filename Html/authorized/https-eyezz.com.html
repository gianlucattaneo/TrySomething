ABOUT EYEZZ

EYEZZ is an online retailer of authentic designer eyewear. Independently owned and operated by licensed eye care professionals, we are proud to offer the best selection of prescription eyeglasses, fashion sunglasses, RX sunglasses and optical frames. EYEZZ is one of the few authorized online retailers of Luxottica products.

Our stock includes over 100 designer brands such as Ray-Ban, Prada, Versace, Gucci, Burberry, DKNY and many others.
EYEZZ.com owns and utilizes a NY based state of the art optical laboratory, which allows us to provide lens fitting services to our customers in addition to allowing us to control and oversee the quality of lenses and product assembly, as well as, provides us with the ability to offer a large variety of lenses, materials and coatings for prescription frames and sunglasses.

While focusing on the US market, EYEZZ.com ships worldwide to over 92 countries.

we help little angels

THE FOUNDER
Anna Smirnova. Co-Founder.

Anna Smirnova, the CEO of Eyezz.com, was forced to launch her eyewear startup under very abnormal circumstances. Her mother was diagnosed with a horrible life altering disease: multiple myeloma – cord blood cancer. This misfortune ruined her family life and left an intolerable scar inside of her heart.

For almost 5 years, Anna had to make many life-changing decisions to fight for her mother’s life. She drained her family’s budget and friend’s savings, including credit cards to pay for anticancer treatments.

While desperate for money, she convinced several random investors to fund her eyewear start-up. Later it would become an official Luxottica distributor working with hundreds of top eyewear brands. This endeavor helped Anna’s family to pay for her mother’s treatments, which, regretfully, had no positive result.

Affected by Anna’s family misfortune, Eyezz.com is dedicated to helping children and cancer research institutions by donating from each sale made on Eyezz.com. Anna strives to help more and more people fight and survive cancer.
