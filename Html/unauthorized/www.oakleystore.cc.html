WHAT TO LOOK FOR IN OAKLEY SUNGLASSES

Before we jump into our lineup of the best Oakley men’s sunglasses, let’s quickly review Oakley proprietary technology and what that means for the frames.

FRAME TECHNOLOGY

Dedicated to providing the best-performing eyewear for virtually any sport or active lifestyle, Oakley engineers have created proprietary frame materials that surpass the competition. For sunglasses, that means O Matter™ frame construction and Unobtainium™ rubber grip. You’ll get lightweight and durable wear with a strategically placed grip that gets tackier when wet and stays perfectly in place no matter how much you break a sweat.

LENS TECHNOLOGY

As for lenses, Oakley PRIZM™ is the star of the show. This contrast-enhancing lens technology calibrates the visible light spectrum to finetune color, contrast, and clarity for your specific environment. Made from Plutonite™, Oakley’s proprietary lens material, all PRIZM™ lenses are impact-resistant and deliver superior optics. And they’re Rx-able!
